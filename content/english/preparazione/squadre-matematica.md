This website does not focus specifically on **mathematics teams**, but in the Treviso district many schools have long participated in team competitions connected to the **Mathematics Olympiad**.

Among the schools that have developed a well-established tradition in this area over the years are, for example:

- State Scientific High School **"Leonardo da Vinci"** in Treviso  
- State Scientific High School **"Marconi"** in Conegliano Veneto  
- State Scientific High School **"Levi"** in Montebelluna  
- State Scientific High School **"Berto"** in Mogliano Veneto  
- State Higher Education Institute **"Planck"** in Villorba  
- State Higher Education Institute **"Scarpa"** in Motta di Livenza  
- State Higher Education Institute **"Fanno"** in Conegliano  
- State Classical High School **"Flaminio"** in Vittorio Veneto  

and many others.  
The list changes over time, as more and more schools in the district take part in **team competitions**, in addition to individual contests.

If your school has a **mathematics team**, we recommend taking advantage of this opportunity.  
Team training sessions are an excellent way to approach olympiad mathematics.

During these meetings students work on problems similar to those used in competitions, but above all they learn to:

- **reason together** with other team members  
- discuss different **strategies** and **ideas**  
- develop **speed** and **intuition** in solving problems  

In addition to being very useful for mathematical preparation, team activities are also **fun and engaging**.

In other words: a different way of doing mathematics, **collaborating and challenging oneself together with others**.
