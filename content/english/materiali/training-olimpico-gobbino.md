Prof. **Massimo Gobbino** is a full professor of Mathematical Analysis at the **University of Pisa**.

For more than twenty-five years he has been involved in the **training, selection, and mentoring of Italian students for the International Mathematical Olympiad (IMO)**.

In 1987 he won the Italian Mathematics Olympiad and in the same year he participated in the **International Mathematical Olympiad**, earning a **bronze medal**.

---

Over the years, **Prof. Massimo Gobbino** has produced a large amount of material for Mathematics Olympiad preparation.  
An important part of this material is collected on the website called **Olympiad Training**.

The website is available here: 🔗 http://pagine.dm.unipi.it/gobbino/Home_Page/OT_Index.html

The site contains several useful resources for olympiad training.

### Problem collection

One of the most useful sections is the **exercise page**, where many problems used during training camps and educational activities are collected.

Much of the material comes from **national training camps** organized within the Mathematics Olympiad project and is very useful for students who want to practice olympiad-level problems.

### Training camp video lectures

Another particularly valuable resource is the collection of **video lectures recorded during various training camps**.

The video lectures are available here: 🎬 http://olimpiadi.dm.unibo.it/videolezioni/

This page includes several collections of lectures, including:

- **StageLocali** ▸ video lectures from local training camps  
- **Training** ▸ video lectures from national training camps  

This is an extremely rich resource: the lessons cover many of the fundamental topics of olympiad mathematics and allow students to follow high-level explanations directly from trainers in the Italian olympiad community.

In short, it is a **very valuable resource for students who want to deepen their olympiad preparation**.

## The “Olympiad Sheets”

Another well-known resource for olympiad preparation is the book **Olympiad Sheets for the Preparation for the Mathematics Olympiads**, written by **Massimo Gobbino**.

- **Olympiad Sheets for the Preparation for the Mathematics Olympiads** ▸ Massimo Gobbino  
  ISBN: 978-88-96336-33-5  
  https://umi.dm.unibo.it/prodotto/schede-olimpiche/ (or available during the national final in Cesenatico)

The book is designed as a compact collection of useful tools for olympiad preparation.  
As the author writes, it is:

> a collection of tools, a 'cheat sheet'.

The text contains, in a very concise form and without proofs:

- statements of important theorems  
- useful formulas  
- recurring tools used in solving olympiad problems  

It is therefore particularly useful as a **quick reference manual** during study or training.

According to many students and trainers in the olympiad community, it is a true **must-have** for anyone who wants to prepare seriously for the competitions.
