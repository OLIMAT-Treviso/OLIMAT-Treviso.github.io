### A series dedicated to mathematics competitions

The U Math series offers a **wide selection of books specifically designed for preparation for mathematics competitions**.

Many volumes focus on specific topics of olympiad mathematics (such as algebra, combinatorics, number theory, or geometry) and have already been presented in the pages of this website dedicated to the various subjects.

Alongside these, there are also several books containing:

- collections of problems  
- training manuals  
- lessons and teaching materials used in preparation for competitions  

These texts are often written by teachers and trainers who have many years of experience preparing students for the Mathematics Olympiad.
