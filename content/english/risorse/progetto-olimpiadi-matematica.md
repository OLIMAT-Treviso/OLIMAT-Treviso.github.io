The official website of the **Mathematics Olympiad Project**, managed by the Italian Mathematical Union, is available here:

🔗 http://olimpiadi.dm.unibo.it

It is the main reference point for all official information related to mathematics competitions in Italy.

On the website it is possible to find:

- information about the **different stages of the competitions**  
- official announcements  
- preparation materials  
- educational resources and video lectures  

---

### Competition problems and solutions

A particularly useful section of the website is the one dedicated to **competitions**.

In the **“Le Gare”** menu you can find the **problem statements from competitions of previous years**, organized by type of competition and by year.  
In many cases the **official solutions** are also available.

These collections include, for example:

- **Archimedes’ Games**  
- **District competitions**  
- **National final in Cesenatico**  
- other competitions connected to the Olympiad project
