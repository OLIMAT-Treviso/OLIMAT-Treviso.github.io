**OliForum** is the official forum of the **Mathematics Olympiad Project** and is one of the main online meeting points for students, teachers, and enthusiasts of olympiad mathematics.

You can access the forum here: 🔗 https://www.oliforum.it

On the forum it is possible to find:

- discussions about **olympiad mathematics topics**  
- problems proposed by users  
- solution attempts and discussions of solving methods  
- clarifications on exercises and techniques  
- exchanges with other students interested in competitions  

It is also a place where you can **ask questions**, propose your own solutions, or request suggestions on specific topics.

Naturally, as with any forum, it is always advisable to read discussions with a critical mindset: solutions proposed by users **are not always correct**, but interesting insights often emerge precisely from discussion.
