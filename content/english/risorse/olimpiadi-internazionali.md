The **International Mathematical Olympiad (IMO)** is the most important international mathematics competition for high school students.

The official IMO website is available here: 🔗 https://www.imo-official.org

On the website it is possible to find extensive information about the competition, its history, and the participating delegations.

---

### IMO problems

From the **“Problems”** menu of the website it is possible to access the complete archive of problems from the International Mathematical Olympiads.

In this section you can find:

- the **problem statements** from all editions of the IMO  
- the possibility to select the **year of the competition**  
- problem statements in **several languages**, including Italian

---

### Very advanced problems

IMO problems are **very challenging** and represent the highest level of mathematics competitions for high school students.

For this reason, they are generally suitable for students who are following a **very advanced preparation path**, such as those who perform well in the **national training camps in Pisa**.

On the official IMO website **solutions to the problems are not provided**.  
This makes the archive particularly interesting for those who want to challenge themselves with extremely difficult problems.
