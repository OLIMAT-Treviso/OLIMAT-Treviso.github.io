We recommend a very useful set of lecture notes for preparing for the Mathematics Olympiads: the **Mathematics Notebook** published by the **Subalpine Mathesis Association – Bettazzi Section**.

- **Olympiad Mathematics Notes** ▸ A. Astolfi, G. Audrito, A. Carigano, F. Tanturri  
  (second edition 2010, revised and expanded by G. Audrito, G. Distefano, R. Maffucci, L. Prelli, F. Roman)  
  https://www.mat.uniroma1.it/sites/default/files/liceomatematico/olimpiadi/dispenseolimpionichetorino.pdf

This text was designed as **support material for students who want to prepare for the Mathematics Championships**, providing a solid theoretical foundation before tackling more advanced problems.

As the authors write:

> We tried to address this book to the widest possible audience of students, maintaining a rigorous approach while avoiding unnecessary formalism and emphasizing practical aspects.

These notes are very clear and accessible, making them particularly suitable also for **students studying independently**.  
For many topics, they can represent **an excellent starting point for beginning the study of olympiad mathematics**.
