Promoted by [**Fondazione Cassamarca**](https://fondazionecassamarca.it/) as part of the **“Thursdays of Culture”**, the conference in Treviso focuses on the value of mathematics as a tool for the growth of younger generations.

Teachers and experts present the experience of the **Mathematics Championships**, highlighting the work of schools in the Treviso area in preparing students and the impact on STEM skills and soft skills.  
An event that showcases talent, education, and learning opportunities within the local community.

Learn more about the cultural initiative [here](https://fondazionecassamarca.it/notizie/quando-la-matematica-e-una-passione-ai-giovedi-della-cultura-giovani-e-matematica-a-treviso/).
