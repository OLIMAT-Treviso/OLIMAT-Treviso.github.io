This policy describes the use of cookies on the website: **www.olimat-tv.it**

The policy is provided in accordance with **Regulation (EU) 2016/679 (GDPR)** and Italian legislation regarding personal data protection.

---

#### What cookies are

Cookies are small text files that websites visited by the user send to the device used (computer, tablet, or smartphone), where they are stored and then transmitted back to the same websites on subsequent visits.

Cookies allow the website to function correctly and, in some cases, improve the browsing experience.

---

#### Data controller

The Data Controller is the **current District Coordinator of the Mathematics Olympiad** for the Treviso district.

For information or reports it is possible to use the contact details available on the website.

---

#### Types of cookies used by the website

This website uses **only technical cookies**.

Technical cookies are necessary for the proper functioning of the website and allow, for example:

- correct display of pages  
- easier navigation between different sections of the site  
- management of certain technical display preferences  

These cookies **do not collect personally identifiable information** and **are not used for profiling or marketing purposes**.

---

#### Profiling cookies and third-party cookies

The website **does not use**:

- profiling cookies  
- advertising cookies  
- user tracking systems  
- traffic analysis tools (analytics)

---

#### Cookies from external services

Some pages of the website may contain links to or embedded content from external services (for example PDF documents, videos, or other educational materials).

Any use of cookies by such services is governed by the **cookie policies of the respective external websites**, over which the site owner has no control.

---

Since the website uses only technical cookies necessary for its functioning, **prior user consent is not required** for their use, although an informational pop-up may still be displayed.

Users may nevertheless manage or disable cookies through their browser settings.
